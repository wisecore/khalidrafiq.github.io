About Me

Hi! My name is KHALID RAFIQ and I would like to thank you for taking the time out and visiting my site.
Since my childhood I have always been interested in science and technology, which lead me to pursue my interest in acquiring an MBA in Information Systems and later professional certifications in Agile Methodology (Scrum Master) & ITIL Ver-3.

In the past I have worked with Oil & Gas, Banking & Insurance and Technology & Telecom industries. Professionally, I started out as a Business Analyst and enjoyed that rewarding career for over six years. Since last three years (2013) I have been gravitating steadily towards Front End Development and found it a very fascinating field. Being more of a visual person myself, I believe I am here to stay with it for a long time to come. I have created some awesome looking websites and a few interesting applications during my smooth transition to web programming (They can be sampled here on the project page).

Currently my title is of a Front End Developer. In this role, I think of my self as a problem solver. I am a firm believer in a ‘CAN DO’ attitude. With experience I have learned that a developer’s thought process must frame itself as to develop solutions to business problems. Thinking globally while acting locally! And my business analysis experience has come in quite handy for me to develop that thinking model.

In addition to that, I also have keen interest in non-fictional reading, particle physics, functional medicine and NLP. I have also developed a discipline of exercising regularly and enjoy traveling during my vacations.
